Learning Journal Five: Best UX for Game Design

Nov-10-2021

This article has expanded my view of game design. Often, when I think about designing the game, storytelling pops up in my mind. Yet, I didn't think about the importance of interface and UX design.

I was too naive!

The comfort-to-reach area, the sliders, pop-ups, and user choices are the parts that guild users to the stories. The change of button position might lead the user to the game and get more involved or quit the game after a couple of unreasonable button positioning. I also learned about dark-UX that where the mistakes might be designed intentionally.

The design based on the data analysis is powerful and can sometimes swing users' behaviors. We must be careful of applying the changes and balancing between the profits and healthy user behaviors.
