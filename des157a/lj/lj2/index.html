Learning Journal Two: Overlay

Oct-20-2021

The overlay is a great way to present extra information, but it often appears to be annoying or useless. The articles mentioned the benefits of overlaying, such as showing temporary information, alert, extra content, or focusing on content. However, using overlay requires a good design and good experience. Do you really need the overlay to display all those information? Maybe just a tiny overlay by the element? In fact, many websites have replaced overlay with a sidebar or another way of interaction in recent years. For example, google image was using an overlay to display the image user clicked, but they have changed the removed overlay and displayed the photos in a sidebar.
