Boost Your Delivery

This is a multiplayer game for 2-4 players. Each of the players will be assigned a delivery truck to start the game. The goal is to get trucks to the destination(total of 100 miles), to do so, players need to boost the mileage for the trucks. The first truck arrives destination wins! Each round, the players have two options:

  1. SPIN the number and move the truck forward by click FILL button.(Spin as many time as you want to get the best score. ONLY the last score will be stored.) However, there are risks:
    • If any of the three numbers is 1, the truck will not for this round
    • If two or more numbers are 1, the truck will break and return to the starting point. So don't do that.
  2. Move the truck forward for 10 miles. (no risk)
